Shipping Policy

At Threaded, we are committed to providing fast and reliable shipping to our customers. Here is an overview of our shipping policy:

  • We offer free standard shipping on all orders within the Pakistan.
  • Orders are typically processed and shipped within 1-2 business days, depending on availability of the items ordered.
  • Expedited shipping options are available at an additional cost. Contact us for more information on expedited shipping rates and delivery times.
  • We ship internationally, but additional fees and restrictions may apply. Contact us for more information on international shipping rates and policies.

If you have any questions or concerns about our shipping policy, please do not hesitate to contact us. We are here to help and make sure your shopping experience is as smooth and enjoyable as possible.
