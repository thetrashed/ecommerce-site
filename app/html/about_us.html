About Us

Threaded is a clothing brand that embodies the spirit of determination and hard work. When we first started out, we had no idea just how far this journey would take us. It all began as a project for a web engineering course, an idea that we were passionate about and wanted to see come to life. As four undergraduate students with a love for fashion and a desire to create something that truly resonated with people, we poured our hearts and souls into this project.

And as it turns out, all of our hard work paid off. The response from our customers was overwhelmingly positive, and it was not long before our brand started to take off. We were amazed by the support we received and were grateful for the opportunity to share our vision with the world.

Today, Threaded is a thriving clothing brand that is dedicated to offering high-quality, stylish pieces that speak to our customers' unique sense of style. We believe in the power of fashion to inspire and empower, and we strive to create pieces that make people feel confident and comfortable.

Our journey has been filled with challenges, but it has also been incredibly rewarding. We are grateful for the opportunity to do what we love every day and to be able to make a positive impact on the world through our brand. We are excited to continue to grow and evolve, and we hope you will be a part of it. Thank you for supporting us on this journey and for choosing to wear Threaded clothing. We could not do it without you.
